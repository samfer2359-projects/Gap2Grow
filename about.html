About Gap2Grow 🌱

Gap2Grow is a smart learning platform designed to help users identify their skill gaps and guide them toward the right learning path. By comparing your current skills with industry requirements, Gap2Grow helps you grow step by step towards your dream career.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students and professionals by providing a clear learning roadmap. Instead of guessing what to learn next, Gap2Grow analyzes your skills and recommends resources that help you build real industry-ready capabilities.

What Gap2Grow Offers

🎯 Skill Gap Analysis

Identify the difference between your current skills and job requirements.

📚 Smart Recommendations

Get curated learning resources tailored to your learning needs.

📈 Track Your Progress

Monitor how your skills improve as you complete learning tasks.

🚀 Career Growth

Follow a structured path to move closer to your dream career.
